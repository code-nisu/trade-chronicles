					Facts about Indian International Trade Fair#
# 
1. The India International Trade Fair, ever since its inception in 1980, has evolved as a major event for the business community.#
#
2. IITF is a major tourist attraction and lakhs of people visit the fair every year. This annual event provides a common platform for the manufacturers, traders, exporters and importers. #
#
3. The fair attracted a huge audience of more than 3 million general visitors & 2,75,000 business visitors including 91 delegations from 53 countries and every year numbers are increasing.#
#
4. Every year, IITF is based on different theme.#
